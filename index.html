Meet Siddharth Warrier

Siddharth Warrier

MBBS, MD (Medicine), D.M (Neurology), TEDx Speaker

Dr. Siddharth Warrier is a Neurologist with a 5-year experience in the field of Neurology. Dr. Warrier completed his MBBS from Grant Medical college and JJ Hospital, Mumbai, and his MD Medicine from Seth G.S. Medical College and KEM Hospital. He completed his D.M (Neurology) at Sanjay Gandhi postgraduate Institute, Lucknow. He worked as a Senior Registrar in the department of Neurology at KEM Hospital from 2019-2020. He completed his MRCP training and received his MRCP UK (London) degree in 2019. He is currently attached to Wockhardt Hospital, Mira Road from February 2020.

Dr. Warrier is a Life Member of Indian Association of Neurology and Maharashtra Association of Neurology. He is a member of the Royal College of Physicians, London (MRCP). He is also a corresponding member of the European Academy of Neurology.
